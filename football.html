    Overview

    This section of Caspedia is dedicated to documenting the break football activity that takes place within our community. It covers current and upcoming matches of real football interest, as well as a full archive of our own recorded break-time football games — played during recess by two teams: HaramBall FC and Al Noor FC.

    Matches are played during school break (recess) and typically last between 15 and 20 minutes, with the exact duration left to the discretion of the appointed referee. The appointed referee is Ameer M. Alnaimi, who may delegate to an assistant referee if he sees fit. All match results and notable in-game events are recorded here for the community's reference.

    It is worth emphasising that these are imaginary, roleplay clubs created entirely for entertainment. They are not officially recognised by the school, do not operate under any school authority, and should not be confused with any official school club or sports programme.

    HaramBall FC

    ⚽ HaramBall FC
    Founded 19 April 2026
    Founder Malek Amro E.
    President Malek Amro E.
    Captain Khalid W.
    Members 8 official + reserves
    Status Active
    Type Recreational / Roleplay

    HaramBall FC was established on 19 April 2026 by Malek Amro E., who serves as both the club's founder and president. The formation of HaramBall FC came directly in response to the creation of Al Noor FC the previous day — Malek, alongside his appointed captain Khalid W., saw an opportunity to form a competing side and bring a new dimension of friendly rivalry to the community's break-time activities.

    The club is composed of eight official members. In addition to its core squad, HaramBall FC operates with a pool of spare reserve players who may be called upon to fill in for either team during a given match, subject to the approval of the referee and the captains or presidents of both sides.

    Al Noor FC

    ⚽ Al Noor FC
    Founded 18 April 2026
    Founder Zaid Majdi
    President Zaid Majdi
    Captain Zaid Majdi
    Members 8 official + reserves
    Status Active
    Type Recreational / Roleplay

    Al Noor FC was established on 18 April 2026 by Zaid Majdi, who holds the roles of both president and captain. Zaid founded the club with the ambition of introducing a new era of organised football during the school's break sessions — bringing structure, team identity, and some competitive spirit to what had previously been informal kickabouts.

    The founding of Al Noor FC directly inspired the creation of HaramBall FC the following day, effectively giving rise to the two-team rivalry that now defines the break football scene within the community. Like its counterpart, Al Noor FC has eight official members and shares the same reserve pool arrangement.
