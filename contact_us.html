    Introduction

    Thank you for your interest in contacting Caspedia. Whether you have spotted an error in an article, have a complaint, or simply want to ask a question, we want to make sure your message reaches the right place and is dealt with appropriately.

    Before reaching out, we ask that you take a moment to read through the information on this page. It will help set the right expectations and point you in the correct direction, which ultimately means a faster and more useful response for you.

    Important Disclaimers

    Please take note of the following before submitting any inquiry or complaint:

    Disclaimer
    ⚖️ Caspedia.uk is not responsible for mistakes made by its editors and will not be held liable for them, unless the content in question constitutes a violation of local cyber laws or the British Online Safety Act.
    🕐 Caspedia currently has one editor — Ameer M. Alnaimi. Please expect delays in responding to inquiries or complaints. We appreciate your patience.
    🌍 Although our domain registrar is based in the United Kingdom of Great Britain, Caspedia is operated from the United Arab Emirates and is subject to UAE cyber laws in addition to applicable UK legislation.
    🏫 Caspedia is not affiliated with any school or educational institution in any capacity. Please do not contact us expecting institutional representation or authority.
    📋 If you have questions about the purpose or background of Caspedia, please visit the About Caspedia page before reaching out — your question may already be answered there.
    ✏️ If you wish to suggest a new article or addition to the site, please use the Suggest an Edit page rather than sending an email.
    🪲 If you wish to report a bug or something unsual, please use the Report a Bug page rather than sending an email.

    How to Contact Us

    For all general inquiries, complaints, or reports of contradictory or false information in any article, please contact us using the email address below. All correspondence is handled by Ameer M. Alnaimi.

    📧 General Inquiries & Complaints

    Use this address for all contact including error reports, complaints, and content concerns.

    INQUIRES_CASPEDIA@TUTA.IO Send Email
    How to write your email: Please clearly state your reason in the subject line — for example, Subject: Complaint or Subject: False Information – [Article Name]. In the body of your message, provide as much detail as possible: which page the issue is on, what the problem is, and any evidence or context you can share. This helps us address your concern as efficiently as possible.
    🚫
    Do not use this email for article suggestions or edit requests. Those should be submitted via the Suggest an Edit page on GitHub.
